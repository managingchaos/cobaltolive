About Cobalt Olive

Cobalt Olive is part design shop, part marketing firm, and part fixer. We help clients clarify, express, and amplify their story—across the channels that matter most. Whether you’re scaling a new venture or steering an established brand through change, we blend creative and strategic thinking to get results with nuance, clarity, and boldness.

What We Do

We provide a blend of strategy, design, and campaign execution. That includes brand positioning, logo development, market research, internal tools, packaging, and fully integrated creative campaigns. We act as collaborators or silent partners—whatever the moment demands.

What We Value

We believe in creative pragmatism—beautiful work that works. We believe every message is a reflection of your brand. We believe in confidentiality, curiosity, and fierce loyalty to the client. And we believe in showing up with solutions, not just slides.
